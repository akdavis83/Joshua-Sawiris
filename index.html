Called to Ignite Revival

Joshua and Jenelle Sawiris serve as the senior Pastors of Glory City Nashville. They have been married for 24 years and in ministry for over two decades, with their three daughters serving alongside them. Commissioned by our advisory board and under the spiritual covering of Dr. Katherine and Tom Ruonala and Prophet James Goll, the Sawiris family was sent to Nashville, Tennessee, in 2024 after receiving multiple prophetic confirmations to ignite revival and awaken hearts across the nation.

Who We Are

We are a five-fold apostolic Church family passionate about raising up, equipping, empowering, and releasing people into the call of God upon their lives.

At Glory City Nashville, we believe that through Jesus' sacrifice, the full penalty of sin, death, and sickness has been paid. As sons and daughters, we are called not only to hear God's voice but to walk in obedience to it.

When we prioritize intimacy and live from our identity as His beloved, the knowledge and love of God increases across the earth.
