Joshua and Jenelle Sawiris serve as the senior Pastors of Glory City Nashville. With over two decades of ministry and 24 years of marriage, they carry a five-fold apostolic calling that burns with passion for the presence of God.

Commissioned by Dr. Katherine and Tom Ruonala and Prophet James Goll, they were sent to Nashville in 2024 to ignite revival and awaken hearts. Joshua carries a strong prophetic and miracle anointing, dedicated to raising up a generation that walks in intimacy with God and releases His kingdom on earth through signs, wonders, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

We are a family passionate about raising up, equipping, empowering, and releasing people into the call of God upon their lives. At Glory City Nashville, we move together in faith, knowing the Lord shows up every time we gather.
